Kingdom Come Rising

Kingdom Come Rising is an epic and humorous RPG inspired by classic JRPGs.

These are the minimum requirements to run it:

  • CPU: Intel Celeron N4000 or Raspberry Pi 4
  • GPU: Intel UHD Graphics 600 or Broadcom VideoCore VI
  • RAM: 2GB or more, 4GB recommended
  • Storage: 500MB or more free space
